About Me

✦

I am actively pursuing web development and programming opportunities to deepen my technical expertise and refine my focus for a future PhD. My approach goes beyond surface-level use of AI; I apply statistical and probabilistic thinking to understand and solve problems using machine learning and data analysis. As a recent graduate entering the professional field, I bring an interdisciplinary perspective that integrates the natural sciences with rigorous, data-driven problem solving.

As of now, the projects I've built have been focused on making everyday tasks easier; my long-term goals are to develop computational methods for understanding biological data, contribute to research on the conservation of insect species, and create online resources to help spread knowledge of insect ecology.
