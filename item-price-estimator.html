Item Price Estimator

The item price estimator is my first app and my first experiment with vibe-coding. The majority of it was coded with the Antigravity App, though I went through the code myself to make the changes needed for the app to function properly. While there are understandable concerns about AI replacing peoples' jobs, I don't think coders will have to worry - the process of building the app was faster, but it took the same amount of time to debug everything!

The function of the app is very simple. Using your device's camera, you take a picture of an object. The app then uses retrieval augmented generation (RAG) to retrieve search results containing the same (or a similar) object; it uses those search results to estimate the price of the object based on its quality. Add a funky psychedelic theme, and the result is a handy, stylish litle gadget that makes pricing your items much easier!

Whether you're organizing a garage sale, getting rid of old furniture, or just curious, the Item Estimator provides a simple way to help you out.
