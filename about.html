Who I Am

As a recent college graduate and new professional, my interests combine both the natural world and the identification of patterns and solutions, using artificial intelligence and other tools to model and better understand that world. I'm specifically focused on the interdependencies of bioniformatics, genetics, and entomology.

As of now, the projects I've built have been focused on making everyday tasks easier; my goal is to create projects that tackle the issues surrounding insect conservation, genetic analysis, and parasite ecology.

What I Do

I've worked with multiple coding languages, and my experience has helped me adapt for each project's different requirements. These include

  • Python
  • Pytorch
  • Pandas
  • SQL
  • Protege
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • RStudio

The AI programs I've had the most experience with are Antigravity, Google AI Studio, Google Gemini, and Claude AI. I also use many of Microsoft Azure's tools to create and train machine learning models, plan and manage AI solutions, and implement said solutions. This includes extensive work with running these apps on Google Cloud Run, creating Dockers, and performing jobs and services.
