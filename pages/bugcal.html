BugCal

✦

BugCal is a personal calendar that's deceptively simple to use. Just type what you want in plain English — "standup every Mon/Wed/Fri at 9am for 30 min" — and BugCal parses the title, time, duration, and recurrence automatically. Prefer to speak? Hit the mic button and say it out loud; the voice agent handles the rest and reads the confirmation back to you.

Five independent AI agents work behind the scenes: one parses free-form text into structured events, one detects schedule conflicts in real time, one verifies US holiday dates against live web results, one interprets voice commands, and one converts times across 20 timezones with awareness of day-boundary crossings.

Five calendar views — Year, Month, Week, Day, and Agenda — let you see your schedule at whatever resolution makes sense. Events can repeat daily, weekly, biweekly, monthly, yearly, or on fully custom day patterns, each with optional end dates or occurrence limits. You can email yourself a reminder for any event, or get a summary of your next 10 upcoming events delivered to your inbox. A powerful search filters by keyword, color, recurrence type, or any of the 12 bug mascots.

Built with

Anthropic Claude API (5 AI agents)
