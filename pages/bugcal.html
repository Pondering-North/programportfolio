BugCal

✦

BugCal is a personal calendar app made with Claude AI. It was a personal test to see how Claude can manage creating multiple agents to handle multiple tasks.

Click any day to add an event, or describe one and let the AI fill in the details. Recurring events, custom repeat patterns, and colour-coded categories are all supported. Events are saved to your browser's local storage.
