Ento-Professo Search 3000

✦

Ento-Professo Search 3000 is a faculty research tool made for personal use. The goal of this app is to find and aggregate the information of faculty from entomology-focused graduate programs, converting the results into into a database that can be searched and exported. To do this, Ento-Professo uses a multi-agent AI pipeline to fetch, parse, and structure faculty information: names, titles, areas of research, contact information, and links to their profile on the actual sites.

The app uses a program called Jina Reader that converts URLs to LLM-friendly input: in this case, by rendering JavaScript-heavy university pages server-side. It then passes the extracted text to Claude to parse out structured faculty records. This ensures that costs are kept low and enables the app to deal with the dynamic formats of university website pages.

The app currently indexes faculty from the University of Arizona, Kansas State University, and the University of Hawaii(CTAHR), but more schools are planned to be added. It also supports searching by faculty name, title, area of research, and email. For personal use, you can export the results as a CSV or JSON file. I currently plan on adding more export formats and the ability to compress results into a drop-down bar to conserve readability.
